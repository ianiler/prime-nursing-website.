Admission Requirements

  • ✔️ O-Level Certificate (Minimum of a Pass in Core subjects, including Mathematic,English Biology & Chemistry)
  • ✔️ A level Certificate is an added advantage
  • ✔️ Two Passport-Sized Photos
  • ✔️ Copy of National ID or Birth Certificate
  • ✔️ Application Fee Receipt

Admission Process

  1. Fill out the application form.
  2. Attach required documents.
  3. Submit the form to the admissions office or email us.
  4. Wait for the review and acceptance letter.
  5. Pay admission fees and confirm enrollment.
